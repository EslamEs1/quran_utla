Our Mission

At Quran Utla, our mission is to deliver education to those who are eager to learn and in need of guidance. To achieve this, we have developed an effective teaching methodology aimed at providing high-quality programs led by dedicated educators.

The Quran Utla team includes over 100 qualified teachers and staff members from across the globe, all holding degrees from prestigious institutions like Al-Azhar University.

With our services, anyone can access online learning with certified male and female tutors, regardless of age, gender, or location. We take pride in offering exceptional education to Muslims worldwide who wish to study Arabic and the Quran.

Our Vision

Our vision is to spread the learning of the Quran through specialized Tutors and make it accessible to all Muslims in all parts of the world through a live online session. We reach out to 1.9 billion Non-Arab Muslims with these courses and also aim to reach out to the 7.7 billion Non-Muslim brothers and sisters with the message of Islam.

Maximum Approach

Proclaim the message of Islam to most people, whether they live in a Muslim country or a non-Muslim country, and convey the message of Allah to all fellow Muslims and non-Muslims, because Islam is for all Mankind.
